Batchly Bee FAQ

Common questions about task batching and productivity.

What is task batching?
Grouping similar tasks together and completing them in focused sessions. Instead of jumping between types of work all day, you dedicate specific times to each. This reduces context switching.
How much time can batching save?
Context switching costs 15-25 minutes of refocus time per switch. By batching, professionals report 30-50% productivity gains.
How do I start batching?
Add tasks to Batchly Bee, organize into batches by type, set time blocks, and use the focus timer. The app tracks progress and shows trends.
Can I use this for different types of work?
Absolutely. Batching works for knowledge work, creative projects, customer support, sales, and more.
Does batching handle urgent tasks?
Yes. Mark tasks as urgent and Batchly Bee prioritizes them separately so you stay responsive without constant interruptions.
