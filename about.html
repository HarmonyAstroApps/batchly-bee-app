About Batchly Bee

We built Batchly Bee because the world's most productive people don't multitask — they batch.

Batchly Bee is an iOS task management app that applies batch processing principles to daily work. Instead of constant context switching, you group similar tasks and crush them in focused sessions.

The Problem We Solve

Multitasking destroys productivity. Every time you switch tasks, your brain needs 15+ minutes to refocus. Batchly Bee eliminates this by helping you group tasks by type, priority, or time block — so you stay in flow.

Batch Processing Works

Factories use batching to maximize efficiency. Software teams use sprints. Top performers batch their work. Batchly Bee brings this proven strategy to your daily task management.

Key Features

  • Smart task grouping by category, priority, or time blocks
  • Focus timer integration for uninterrupted work sessions
  • Visual task organization with drag-and-drop batching
  • Productivity insights and batch completion stats
  • Syncs across your devices automatically

Our Mission

Help professionals reclaim their focus and triple their output by replacing multitasking chaos with intentional batch processing.
