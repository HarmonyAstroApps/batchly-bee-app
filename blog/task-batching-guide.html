The Complete Task Batching Guide

Short answer: Identify your task types, create time blocks for each, and stick to one batch per block. That's it. Simple but powerful.

Step 1: Identify Your Task Types

What types of work do you do daily? Common examples:

  • Email and messages
  • Phone calls and meetings
  • Creative/focused work (writing, coding, design)
  • Administrative tasks
  • Planning and strategy

Step 2: Create Time Blocks

Assign specific times to each batch type. Example:

  • 9:00-9:30: Email batch
  • 10:00-12:00: Focused work
  • 12:00-1:00: Lunch
  • 1:00-2:00: Admin batch
  • 2:00-4:00: Focused work

Step 3: Use Focus Tools

During each batch, silence notifications from other task types. Focus completely. Use Batchly Bee to organize and track progress.

Step 4: Review and Adjust

After a week, review. Did your batches work? Adjust as needed. Some people batch by day (Monday = admin, Tuesday = calls). Find what works for you.
